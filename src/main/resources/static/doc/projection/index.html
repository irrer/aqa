Projection While all test results are reported in the isoplane in mm, measurements are performed in the image plane. Positions are calculated by using pixels' dimension and center position in the image plane and using the SID and SAD of the RTIMAGE. Note that the SID and SAD might vary between images within a series. The variation is generally very small, but is taken into account. Also, image pixel coordinates are start at 0,0 for the upper left hand side of the image, while isoplane coordinates 0,0 are at the center of the image.
